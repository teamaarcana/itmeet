Our project is a platform which supports in sustainability of social and environmental aspects of SDGs. Our initiative is to help the people who are in crisis of the resources and to motivate the people to donate resources which can be reused like books, clothes and also to motivate people to volunteer themselves in the events like blood donation, sanitation programs, event management etc. Due to this, the needy gets the resources they require and the donors also get the reward points that can be used to redeem discount in different shops.
